Pizza

Description

This is offically and unequivocally the greatest pizza recipe ever created. First starting with the finest frozen dough. Then ragu pizza sauce. Then great value cheese. Then Hormel pepperoni

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Ragu pizza sauce
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Hormel pepperoni
Steps
  1. Flatten dough in pan.
  2. Sauce pizza dough.
  3. Put cheese on.
  4. Put pepperoni on.
  5. Cook at 15500 degrees for 30 minutes
